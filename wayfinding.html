EGD / Wayfinding System
WORK IN PROGRESS > Directing those in motion by organizing &
communicating a navigation system in the School of Art & Art History.

Shapes that connect & combine
into one main shape, each
representing a certain
floor in collaboration with
all majors of the art school.

Line breaks express the concept of space for
creativity & individualism in each student & professor.

Transparent doors & opaque signing allow
us to see the other side of things to further
emphasis communion and understanding.


Interactive touch screens
placed in the lobby show
class & faculty directories
as well as room availability.
